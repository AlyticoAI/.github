# Code of Conduct

AlyticoAI repositories are a professional workspace. Everyone interacting in
issues, pull requests, or discussions is expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in feedback.
- Assume good intent; disagree on technical merits, not personally.
- Keep discussion on-topic and in English for shared visibility.
- Not share credentials, customer data, or respondent PII in issues, PRs,
  commit messages, or logs — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Violations should be reported to a Code Owner or org admin. Repeated or
serious violations may result in removal from the organization.
